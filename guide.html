Welcome !

Thank you for downloading KANOPE !

Before you can browse (mostly) anonymously on the Web,
read the section about Javascript and Cookies and you should be good to go !
Don't forget to disable plug-ins as well !

Our extension only blocks some trackers, this guide will explain to you
on how to configure Opera to only allow your favorite websites scripts and cookies.

Javascript, if enabled, will most likely break your anonymity
and will give lots of info about your computer to the webhoster
like how much Fonts you have installed on your computer,
your tab history and much more...
Only allow it on your favorites websites !

Cookies can be used for tracking by other websites.
Again, we recommend to allow cookies only on your favorites websites.

We are blocking third party cookies by default but some websites might need it.
You can disable this behaviour in Settings.


If you already know this stuff and got TOR working then you can ignore this guide.
