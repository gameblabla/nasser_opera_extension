Thank you for downloading KANOPE !

Before you can browse (mostly) anonymously on the Web ,
read the section about Javascript and Cookies and you should be good to go !
Don't forget to disable plug-ins as well !

  • Configure Javascript
  • Configure Cookies


    • JAVASCRIPT
    'O' logo in the top left -> Settings --> Websites


    Then tick "Do not allow any site to run Javascript"

    Then click on 'Manage Exceptions'


    A window like this should appear.

    You need to enter one by one every site that needs Javascript.
    You go on Youtube often ? Enter youtube.com and choose 'Allow'.
    Press Enter to validate.
    I recommend that you allow 'addons.opera.com' as well or else you won't be able to download other extensions !
    After a while , it should look like this :

    COOKIES

     'O' logo in the top left -> Settings --> Privacy & Security


    Then tick under Cookies "Block sites from settings any data"



    Then click on 'Manage Exceptions'




    A window like this should appear.
    Cookies can used for tracking purposes too which is why
    you should allow only the websites you think are safe.
    You use Facebook every day ? Type 'facebook.com' and select 'Clear on exit'
